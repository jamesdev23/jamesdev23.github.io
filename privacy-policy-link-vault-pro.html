Privacy Policy

Introduction

Link Vault Pro is a mobile application designed to help you securely store, organize, and manage your links. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the confidentiality of your data.

Data Collection and Use

Link Vault Pro does not collect any personal data or device information. All data, including your links, encoded/decoded links, and any associated notes or descriptions, are stored exclusively on your device. We do not transmit or store any of your data on our servers.

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Your link information is stored locally on your device, keeping it under your control. Please be aware that uninstalling the app or resetting your device may result in the loss of your saved data. We strongly recommend regularly backing up your device to prevent data loss.

Children's Privacy

Link Vault Pro is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

 

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the updated   policy within the app or through other communication channels.
